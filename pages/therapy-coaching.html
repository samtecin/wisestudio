How WiseStudio Empowers Therapists & Coaches

WiseStudio is an all-in-one, smart management platform built especially for therapists, counselors, life coaches, and mental health professionals. Whether you provide individual therapy, group counseling, life coaching, or specialized therapeutic services, WiseStudio helps you manage everything from appointment scheduling to secure client records—so you can focus entirely on your clients' healing and growth.

It functions like a professional digital assistant, securely managing your client sessions, treatment notes, payments, and administrative tasks, all with complete confidentiality and compliance.

1. Professional Session Scheduling

  • ✓ Manage individual therapy sessions, group counseling, and specialized treatment programs with a professional calendar system
  • ✓ Schedule multiple therapists and treatment rooms across one or multiple practice locations from one secure dashboard
  • ✓ Send automatic appointment reminders to clients with HIPAA-compliant messaging

2. Secure Online & In-Person Sessions

  • Teletherapy: Seamlessly integrate with secure platforms like Zoom Pro or specialized therapy platforms for confidential online sessions
  • In-Person Practice: Manage office visits, group therapy sessions, and workshops with proper client check-in and attendance tracking

3. Professional Practice Website (White Label)

  • ✓ Establish your professional online presence with WiseStudio integrated into your practice website in under 30 minutes
  • ✓ Complete white-label solution featuring your practice name, credentials, and branding—no WiseStudio visibility
  • ✓ Clients can book appointments, access intake forms, and make payments through your professional portal

4. Secure Client Records Management

  • ✓ Maintain confidential client records including treatment history, session notes, assessment scores, and progress tracking
  • ✓ Securely document therapy goals, treatment plans, and client outcomes with professional-grade data protection

5. Multi-Therapist Practice Management

  • ✓ Assign specific specializations and client caseloads to different therapists and counselors
  • ✓ Seamlessly manage associate therapists, interns, and administrative staff as your practice expands

6. Flexible Treatment Plans & Session Packages

  • ✓ Create customized treatment packages (8-session programs, monthly therapy plans, intensive treatment packages)
  • ✓ Automatically track treatment progress and send renewal notifications when packages near completion

7. Professional Payment Processing

  • ✓ Accept secure payments through multiple methods including UPI, cards, and insurance copays
  • ✓ All payments go directly to your practice account with no commission or transaction fees
  • ✓ Complete financial control with 100% revenue retention

8. Transparent Professional Model

  • ✓ Simple subscription-based pricing with no hidden fees or revenue sharing
  • ✓ Access all professional features regardless of practice size—from solo practitioners to group practices
  • ✓ Designed for therapeutic professionals who value financial transparency

9. Multi-Location Practice Support

  • ✓ Manage multiple therapy locations or offer services across various specialties—all centrally controlled
  • ✓ Track performance metrics, client outcomes, and revenue across each location or therapeutic specialty

10. Clinical Analytics & Outcome Tracking

  • ✓ Access insights into client engagement, treatment effectiveness, session attendance patterns, and practice growth
  • ✓ Use data-driven insights to improve treatment outcomes and optimize your therapeutic practice
