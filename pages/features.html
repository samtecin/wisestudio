A powerful, easy-to-use business platform designed specifically for yoga studios and schools

Class & Session Management

  • Manage multiple classes from one place
  • Admin can manage their free time and schedule one-to-one (private) sessions
  • Admin can manage their practice sessions
  • Admin can manage programs and workshops
  • Control how many clients can subscribe to each type of session

  • Manage multiple membership tiers
  • Add members to specific tiers
  • Grant attendance access to members
  • Pause and resume memberships

Payment & Finance

  • Supports RazorPay and Stripe
  • Create discount coupons with min/max value (flat or %)
  • Enable early bird discounts
  • Use dynamic pricing
  • Clients can view their transaction history
  • Admin can add balance to client wallet

Subscription & Booking

  • Clients can subscribe to private sessions, programs, workshops, and practice sessions
  • Admin can book subscriptions on behalf of clients
  • Add clients and manage waitlists
  • Automated email notifications on booking

User Access & Role Management

  • Role-based access control for admin team

Reports & Analytics

  • Generate dynamic reports
  • Track attendance and scores for clients

Notifications & Communication

  • Customizable email templates
  • Send push notifications to clients

Onboarding & Personalization

  • Smooth onboarding experience for admins and clients
  • Clients can update their personal profiles
  • Admins can update class and profile settings

Space & Availability

  • Admins can share available spaces for sessions

Data Privacy & Compliance

  • Client data is secure and never used externally
  • Data is deleted when admin leaves the platform
