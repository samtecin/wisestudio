How Wise Wellness Studio Works

Get started in just a few simple steps

1

Create Your Profile

Set up your professional profile with photos, bio, credentials, and services offered. Customize your profile to reflect your unique brand and expertise.

2

Schedule Your Classes

Add your availability and create class offerings. Set different pricing for individual sessions, packages, or memberships. Our smart scheduling handles timezones automatically.

3

Share Your Booking Link

Share your personalized booking page via social media, email, or website. Clients can view your availability and book sessions directly.

4

Conduct Sessions & Get Paid

Host live sessions through our integrated video platform or in-person. Payments are processed automatically with UPI, cards, or other methods, with payouts to your bank account.

5

Grow Your Business

Use our marketing tools, client management features, and analytics to expand your reach and optimize your offerings based on real data.
